## Product Hunt Submission

### Title
**GemFlush** — AI-powered visibility audits for local businesses

### Tagline
Measure your online discoverability with objective, competitive data

### Description
GemFlush helps local service businesses understand their online visibility compared to competitors. The platform provides AI-powered audits that analyze search presence, directory listings, and discoverability metrics across multiple platforms.

Unlike subjective SEO assessments, GemFlush delivers quantifiable data showing exactly where visibility gaps exist. This enables businesses—particularly medical practices, legal firms, real estate agencies, and marketing agencies—to prioritize marketing investments based on evidence rather than assumptions.

**Key Features:**
- AI-powered visibility analysis across search engines and directories
- Competitive benchmarking against market competitors
- Objective, quantifiable metrics (not subjective assessments)
- Industry-specific insights for local service businesses

### Maker Comment
Built to solve a real problem we experienced: knowing your online presence needs improvement, but not having objective data to prioritize what matters most. GemFlush provides that missing piece—evidence-based visibility insights that help businesses make smarter marketing decisions.
