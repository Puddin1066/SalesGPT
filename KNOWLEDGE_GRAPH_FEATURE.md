# Knowledge Graph Publishing Feature

## Overview

Your AI Visibility SaaS platform includes **knowledge graph publishing** as a core feature to actively raise AI visibility for healthcare clinics.

## What is Knowledge Graph Publishing?

Knowledge graphs are structured databases that AI systems (ChatGPT, Google AI, medical AI assistants) use to find and understand information. By publishing clinic information to knowledge graphs, clinics become discoverable when patients search using AI tools.

## Value Proposition

### For Clinics

**Active Visibility Improvement**: Unlike passive SEO, knowledge graph publishing actively places clinic information where AI systems look for it.

**AI-Powered Discovery**: When patients ask AI assistants (ChatGPT, Google AI, etc.) "find a dermatologist near me," clinics with knowledge graph presence appear in results.

**Competitive Advantage**: Most clinics aren't in knowledge graphs - this gives early adopters a significant visibility edge.
